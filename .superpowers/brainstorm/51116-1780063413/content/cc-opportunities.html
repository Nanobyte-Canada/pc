Covered Call Opportunities — Two Approaches

Both options use dynamic tickers from actual account positions (no hardcoding).

A Same Grid — CC Opportunity Row per Ticker
Tickers you own 100+ shares of get a CC badge in the ticker column showing available contracts. The "+" slots for those tickers open the quotes screen filtered to calls instead of puts. Active CC positions and CSP positions live in the same grid — no separation.

How it works:
Option A: CC badges inline — simpler, everything in one grid. TQQQ shows both its CSP positions and a "Sell CC" slot in the same row. Good when most tickers have both options and shares.
Option B: Separated sections — active positions stay clean and uncluttered. CC opportunities below act like a "what could you do?" suggestion area. TQQQ appears in both sections (positions above, CC opportunities below). Good when you have many CC-eligible tickers that would otherwise clutter the positions grid.
