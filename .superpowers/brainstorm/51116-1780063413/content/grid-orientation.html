Key differences:
A (current): Expiry rows let you scan "what's expiring soon?" across all tickers at once. Good for DTE urgency.
B (transposed): Ticker rows let you scan one ticker's full timeline left→right. Scales better horizontally as you add expiry dates. Good for per-ticker management.
