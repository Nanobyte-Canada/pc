v2 — Connection flow updates:

  • No "Connect Broker" button — removed from header. Available Brokers section is the primary action area.
  • Available Brokers are clickable — tapping a broker card initiates the connection flow (opens ConnectBrokerDialog).
  • Broker cards show connection state: green dot + "1 Account Connected" for active brokers, amber "Reconnect" button for expired tokens.
  • Section order: Available Brokers first (primary action), Connected Accounts below (management).
  • Mobile: 3-column compact broker grid at top, stacked account cards below. Broker cards show "1 linked" / "Reconnect" pills.
  • Account Detail page unchanged from v1 (breadcrumb, KPIs, tabs).
