Design Decisions

  • Desktop: Integrated search bar in header row. Quote bar as a full-width strip below. Strategy selector as pill buttons. Two-column layout: chain table (flex-grow) + leg builder sidebar (240px fixed).
  • Options chain: Bidirectional layout — Calls left, Strike center, Puts right. ATM row highlighted with emerald left border + tinted background. Bid prices in green (buy), ask prices in red (sell) — clickable to add legs.
  • Leg builder: Compact cards with BUY/SELL + CALL/PUT badges. Strike, mid price, expiry/DTE. X button to remove. "Calculate P&L" button at bottom.
  • P&L panel: 2x2 metrics grid (Max Profit, Max Loss, Net Debit, Risk/Reward) + mini payoff diagram with breakeven markers.
  • Mobile: Calls/Puts toggle (show one side at a time). Simplified 4-column chain (Strike, Bid, Ask, Delta). Floating "2 Legs Selected" + Calculate bar at bottom. Leg details and P&L open as bottom sheets when tapped.
