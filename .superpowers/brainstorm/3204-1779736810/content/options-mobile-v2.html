Design Decisions

  • Two dropdowns side by side: Strategy (with layers icon) and Expiry (with calendar icon). Both show current selection + chevron. Equal width. Clean, consistent, no scrolling.
  • P&L as bottom sheet: After tapping Calculate, a bottom sheet slides up over the chain with a drag handle. Background dims. Sheet contains:
    • Leg summary cards (side by side)
    • Payoff diagram with breakeven marker, shaded profit/loss zones
    • 2x2 metric grid (Max Profit green-tinted, Max Loss red-tinted, Net Debit, Risk/Reward)
    • Breakeven row with marker dot and value
  • Dismissible: Swipe down on handle or tap X to close the sheet and return to chain.
